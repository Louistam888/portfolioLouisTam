Winters Instruments Latin America website

Official website for Winters Instruments' Latin America division. Built completely solo while juggling my duties as a marketing manager with this company, this website was constructed with a REACT front-end, a WordPress back-end, and GraphQL. This unique stack was chosen to leverage budgeted resources, navigate staffing considerations and to facilitate fast development. Beyond the technical challenges of managing back/front-end integration, caching and engineering technical architecture, this project was a major test of personal resilience in the face of uncertainty, adaptability, and effective internal advocacy for project survival to completion. This site was well received by the Latin America sales team.
